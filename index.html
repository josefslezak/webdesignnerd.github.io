What I Do

List of My Services

Website Design

Modern great looking web design. Clean and well organized layout. Optimized for mobile devices (responsive design). Fast loading - necessary for good user experience and SEO.

Website Fixes

Do you already have a website but it looks like it's from 1990s? Does the whole layout break when you access it from your phone? Do you want to add new features and functions?

Website Maintenance

Do you need to upgrade your WordPress website or do you need to install new plugins? Do you want to migrate your website to different hosting account? Do you want to backup your whole website?

Speeding Up Website

Based on study 53% of visits are abandoned if a mobile site takes longer than three seconds to load. WordPress websites suffer from slow loading speed when too many plugins are installed.

Graphic Design

Do you need banners or posters for your business? Or business cards or new graphic elements for your website?

Digital Photography

Do you need pictures of your products or your work? Or do you need pictures and videos for your social media accounts?
