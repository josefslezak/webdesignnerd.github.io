More Customers for Your Business

I get it. You want more customers and clients for your business. That's why you want a website. Website which fails to do those things for your business doesn't brong much value.

There are several things every website must have to be successful. It's great looking well organized design. It's mobile optimization so your clients can access it from laptops, smartphones and tablets. It's fast loading time. And the most important thing - visitors!

How do you get visitors to your website? That's the most difficult part of every website owner. I'll show you how I'll it with your website.

First I'm going to do keyword research for your business so we can find your target keywords.

What are your target keywords?

Let say you're a plumber from Asheville. Then your target keyword is "plumber Asheville" because this is the most propable keyword your prospects will be seacrhing on Google.

I'm going to find more keywords related to your business and then I'm going to optimize our website for these keywords. This process is called Search Engine Optimization (SEO), I'm sure you've heart that phrase before.

So What is Seacrh Engine Optimization?

Simply said Searcrh Engine Optimization helps to tweak your website so Google can better pick up your website when your prospects seacrh for your target keywords on Google.

Get Your Business on Google Map And Improve Trustworhotness of Your Website

Another step is to create your Google My Business account (GMB) and get your business on the Google map. If you get a lot of review your business can get to the local 3-pack.

Local 3-pack is the best three businesses (accoeding to Google) found for the searched keyword

How can you get your business to the local 3-pack?

The most important thing is the number of reviews. After every complete job ask your client to write you a review on your GMB profile. That will help you to climb up in the results.

I know what you're thinking.

How can you get your business to the top... righ?

The main factor for the high ranking is number of the GMB reviews. Get a review everytime you finish a job for a client and you're on your way to the local 3-pack.

Local Business Directories

Now it's time to add your business to the local business directories. Yelp, Yellow Pages,... This is great for two reasons.

1. Getting your business to local business directories will connect you with the communities created around these websites.

2. Google will see all the links pointing from those directories to your website which help your website with SEO (backlinks).
