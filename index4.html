7 Thing You Need to Know Before You Hire a Website Designer

Website without Traffic doesn't bring you any business. Your website is like a store in an abandoned part of the town. You can make it look great but if you don't bring customers then you won't make any sales. How can you bring visitors to your website? You need to promote it. Use local business business directories, search engine optimization, your business Facebook page or create an email list.

Outdated old looking website will most likely turn off your visitors. Your website is a reflection of your business. Make it look fresh and modern. Make sure all parts of your website work properly. Make sure your website shows correctly on smartphones and tablets. Make it as easy as possible for your customers to learn about your services and products, contact you and make a purchase.

You can't simply pay Google to put your website on the first page of the Google results. Order of the webites in Google results is based on many factors. Google wants to makes sure that the websites on teh first page are the highest quality ones. How does Google measure quality of a website? They look at the overall presence of the business on the Internet. They look at the reviews of the customers, how old is the website? Is the business an authority in the area? How long do visitors stay on the website? And many others.

How Can You Make Your Website Work for Your Business?

This is how you can build your website if you're on a budget but still want a website which works.

Get a Mobile Optimized website with on page SEO optimization for your keywords.

More than half of all visitors on the internet today use their phone and tablet as a main browsing device. Therefore your website must be well optimized for all kinds of devices and screen so your website displays correctly on all sizes of screen.

On page SEO optimization means that titles, text and other elements of your website are include the keywords you're trying to target. For example if you're a plumber from Asheville your main title on the front page should include Asheville Plumber keywords.
